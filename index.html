Quiz on "I Feel at Home Already!"

Summary

The lesson "I feel at home already!" focuses on a family preparing their home for a visit from their grandmother. The family, the Qadris, live in London, and they have been working for a week to get the house ready. Tasks include buying a new carpet, setting up a wardrobe, putting up curtains, and arranging flowers. They face a tight deadline to complete everything before Grandma's arrival. The lesson covers vocabulary related to household items and activities, along with expressions used for making preparations.
